Weiran Yang

weiran.yang@rwth-aachen.de

Hi, I am Weiran Yang ("Apine"). I am pursuing my master degree in Media Informatics at RWTH Aachen University, Germany. My reseach interests include process mining, machine learning and sport analytics.

Currently I am working at Fraunhofer FIT (Fraunhofer-Institut für Angewandte Informationstechnik) in the project Cortado team. and also working for the Institute of Exercise Training and Sport Informatics at Germany Sport University Cologne (Deutsche Sporthochschule Köln, DSHS).

I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2020.
